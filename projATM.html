Nexus ATM
The Nexus ATM is a Java-based project developed during our first year of college as a requirement for our Object-Oriented Programming course. The name Nexus was chosen to reflect the role of banks as a central link between individuals and their financial assets. This project was entirely hard-coded using Visual Studio Code, showcasing our foundational understanding of object-oriented principles, Java programming, and basic system simulation.
